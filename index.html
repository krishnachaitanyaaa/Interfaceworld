Cent account

A cent account is a type of trading account offered by some Forex brokers. It is designed to allow traders to participate in the foreign exchange market with smaller position sizes and lower risk compared to standard trading accounts.

Leverage

Leverage in trading refers to the use of borrowed funds (provided by the broker) to increase the potential return on an investment. It allows traders to control larger positions in the market with a smaller amount of capital. Leverage is expressed as a ratio, such as 1:100, 1:200, or 1:500, which indicates the amount of capital a trader can control compared to their own investment.

No requotes

"No requotes" is a term commonly used in Forex trading to indicate that a broker guarantees the execution of trades without requoting the requested price. A requote occurs when the broker is unable to execute a trade at the price requested by the trader and offers a new price instead, usually due to market volatility or liquidity issues.
